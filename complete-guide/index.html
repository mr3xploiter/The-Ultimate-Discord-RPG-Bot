Getting Started

Welcome to PixelMon, a comprehensive Discord bot that brings a Pok√©mon-inspired RPG experience to your server! This guide will help you understand how to use the bot and make the most of its features.

Adding the Bot to Your Server

To add PixelMon to your Discord server, click the "Add to Discord" button at the top of this page or on the homepage. You'll need to have the "Manage Server" permission in your Discord server to add the bot.

Command Prefix

PixelMon supports two ways to use commands:

  • Slash Commands: Type / followed by the command name (e.g., /daily)
  • Prefix Commands: Type "pixel" followed by the command name (e.g., pixel daily)

Getting Help

If you need help with the bot, you can use the /help command to see a list of all available commands. You can also specify a category to see more detailed information about specific commands.

/help - Shows all command categories
/help economy - Shows all economy commands

Basic Gameplay Loop

Here's a quick overview of what you can do with PixelMon:

  1. Earn coins through daily rewards, work, and other activities
  2. Hunt for items and animals
  3. Mine for resources
  4. Build and upgrade your farm
  5. Collect and train animals
  6. Battle other players and their animals
  7. Complete quests and earn achievements
  8. Rise through the ranks and compete on leaderboards

Command List

PixelMon offers over 50 commands across various categories. Below is a comprehensive list of all commands, organized by category, with detailed explanations of what each command does and how to use it.

Note:

All commands can be used with either the slash command format (/command) or the prefix format (pixel command). This guide will use the slash command format for simplicity.

Economy Commands

Economy commands help you earn and manage your coins, which are the primary currency in PixelMon.

Daily

/daily

Description: Claim your daily coins and XP reward.

Cooldown: 24 hours

Rewards: 100-199 coins and 10-29 XP

Usage: Simply use the command to claim your daily reward. Make sure to claim it every day to maximize your earnings!

/daily

Work

/work

Description: Work to earn coins and XP.

Cooldown: 1 hour

Rewards: 15-80 coins (depending on the job) and XP (approximately 1/3 of coins earned)

Usage: Use this command to work and earn coins. You'll be assigned a random job each time, with different coin rewards.

/work

Balance

/balance

Description: Check your balance, level, and battle record.

Cooldown: 3 seconds

Usage: Use this command to view your current coin balance, level, XP progress, and battle record.

/balance

Gift

/gift [user] [amount]

Description: Gift coins to another user.

Cooldown: 30 minutes

Usage: Use this command to gift coins to another user. You need to specify the user and the amount of coins you want to gift.

/gift @username 100

Note: You cannot gift coins to yourself or to bots, and you must have enough coins to gift.

Battle Commands

Battle commands allow you to challenge other users to battles, either directly or with your animals.

Battle

/battle [opponent]

Description: Battle another user.

Cooldown: 10 minutes

Rewards: 20-49 coins and 10-24 XP for the winner, 1/3 of the XP for the loser

Usage: Use this command to challenge another user to a battle. The outcome is determined by your levels, XP, and a bit of randomness.

/battle @username

Note: You cannot battle yourself or bots.

Animal Battle

/animalbattle [opponent]

Description: Battle your animal against another user's animal.

Cooldown: 10 minutes

Rewards: XP for the winning animal, with a small amount for the losing animal

Usage: Use this command to have your selected animal battle against another user's selected animal. The outcome is determined by the animals' stats, traits, and a bit of randomness.

/animalbattle @username

Note: Both you and your opponent must have a selected animal with at least 20% health.

Game Commands

Game commands offer various mini-games and activities to earn coins, XP, items, and animals.

Hunt

/hunt

Description: Hunt for items and animals.

Cooldown: 30 minutes

Rewards: Various items and a chance to find animals, plus 5-14 XP (1-3 XP if unsuccessful)

Usage: Use this command to hunt for items and animals. You have a chance to find various items like potions, balls, and rare animals.

/hunt

Note: Animals found through hunting need to be added to your farm using the /addanimal command.

Mine

/mine

Description: Mine for resources and animals.

Cooldown: 10 minutes

Rewards: Various resources (stone, iron, gold, diamond, etc.), a chance to find animals, plus 5-14 XP

Usage: Use this command to mine for resources that can be used to build and upgrade structures on your farm. You also have a chance to find animals.

/mine

Note: There's a small chance to hit a jackpot and find a large amount of rare resources.

Guess

/guess [number]

Description: Guess a number between 1 and 10.

Cooldown: 5 minutes

Rewards: 10-29 coins and 3-10 XP if correct, 1 XP if incorrect

Usage: Use this command to guess a number between 1 and 10. If you guess correctly, you'll earn coins and XP.

/guess 7

Gamble

/gamble [amount]

Description: Gamble your coins for a chance to win more.

Cooldown: 10 minutes

Rewards: 1.5-2.0x your bet if you win, plus XP (5% of bet); small consolation XP if you lose

Usage: Use this command to gamble a specific amount of coins. You have a 45% chance to win and a 55% chance to lose.

/gamble 100

Note: Gambling is risky! Only gamble what you can afford to lose.

Animal Commands

Animal commands allow you to manage your animals, including viewing, adding, healing, breeding, and sending them on tasks.

Animals

/animals [subcommand]

Description: View and manage your animals.

Cooldown: 3 seconds

Subcommands:

  • list - List all your animals
  • info [name] - View detailed information about an animal
  • select [name] - Select an animal as your active animal
  • rename [current] [new] - Rename one of your animals
/animals list
/animals info FluffyTail
/animals select FluffyTail
/animals rename FluffyTail MightyTail

Add Animal

/addanimal [type] [name]

Description: Add an animal to your farm.

Cooldown: 5 seconds

Usage: Use this command to add an animal from your inventory to your farm. You need to specify the type of animal and a name for it.

/addanimal Rabbit FluffyTail

Note: You must have the animal in your inventory (from hunting or mining) and have enough space on your farm.

Heal

/heal [animal] [item]

Description: Heal one of your animals.

Cooldown: 5 seconds

Usage: Use this command to heal an animal using a potion. You need to specify the animal's name and the potion to use.

/heal FluffyTail Potion

Note: Different potions heal different amounts of health: Potion (30 HP), Super Potion (70 HP), Max Potion (full health).

Breed

/breed [parent1] [parent2] [name]

Description: Breed two of your animals to create a new one.

Cooldown: 1 hour

Usage: Use this command to breed two of your animals and create a new offspring. You need to specify the names of both parent animals and a name for the offspring.

/breed FluffyTail MightyPaw BabyHop

Note: Both parent animals must be at least level 3 and have at least 50% health. Breeding reduces the parents' health by 20%.

Send

/send [animal] [task]

Description: Send an animal on a task.

Cooldown: 5 seconds

Tasks: hunt (10 minutes), mine (15 minutes), explore (20 minutes)

Usage: Use this command to send an animal on a task to earn rewards. You need to specify the animal's name and the task.

/send FluffyTail hunt

Note: The animal must have at least 30% health to go on a task. Different tasks have different durations and rewards.

Collect

/collect [animal]

Description: Collect rewards from an animal's completed task.

Cooldown: 3 seconds

Usage: Use this command to collect rewards from an animal that has completed its task. You need to specify the animal's name.

/collect FluffyTail

Note: The animal must have completed its task before you can collect rewards.

Animal Top

/animaltop [sort]

Description: View the top animals globally.

Cooldown: 10 seconds

Sort Options: strength, level, wins, power

Usage: Use this command to view the top animals across all users, sorted by your chosen criteria.

/animaltop strength

Farm Commands

Farm commands allow you to manage your farm, including viewing, upgrading, and building structures.

Farm

/farm [subcommand]

Description: View and manage your farm.

Cooldown: 5 seconds

Subcommands:

  • view - View your farm
  • upgrade - Upgrade your farm to the next level
  • build [structure] - Build or upgrade a structure on your farm

Structures: animalHouse, feedingStation, trainingGround

/farm view
/farm upgrade
/farm build animalHouse

Note: Upgrading your farm increases your animal capacity and unlocks new structures. Building structures provides various benefits to your animals.

Shop

Usage: Use this command to view the available items in the shop, along with their prices and descriptions.

Buy

Description: Buy an item from the shop.

Cool Buy an item from the shop.

Cooldown: 3 seconds

Usage: Use this command to buy an item from the shop. You need to specify the item number (from the shop list) and optionally the quantity (default is 1).

Clan Commands

Clan commands allow you to create, join, and manage clans with other users.

Clan

/clan [subcommand]

Description: Manage your clan.

Cooldown: 5 seconds

Subcommands:

  • create [name] [tag] [description] - Create a new clan
  • join [name] - Join an existing clan
  • leave - Leave your current clan
  • info [name] - View information about a clan
  • members - View the members of your clan
  • kick [user] - Kick a member from your clan (leader only)
  • leaderboard - View the clan leaderboard
/clan create "Dragon Tamers" DT "A clan for dragon enthusiasts"
/clan join "Dragon Tamers"
/clan info (view your own clan)
/clan info "Dragon Tamers" (view another clan)
/clan leaderboard

Note: Clan names must be 3-20 characters, and tags must be 2-5 characters. Only clan leaders can kick members.

Gameplay Guide

Getting Started

When you first start using PixelMon, you'll want to focus on earning coins and XP to level up. Here's a recommended path for beginners:

  1. Use /daily to claim your daily reward
  2. Use /work every hour to earn more coins
  3. Use /hunt to find items and animals
  4. Use /mine to gather resources
  5. Use /addanimal to add animals to your farm
  6. Use /battle to battle other users and earn more coins and XP

Economy System

The economy in PixelMon revolves around coins, which you can earn through various activities:

  • Daily rewards: 100-199 coins every 24 hours
  • Work: 15-80 coins every hour
  • Battles: 20-49 coins per win
  • Gambling: Potential to multiply your coins (but risky)
  • Quests: Various coin rewards for completing quests

Leveling and Ranks

As you earn XP, you'll level up and progress through the rank tiers:

  • XP is earned from most activities: daily rewards, work, battles, hunting, mining, etc.
  • Each level requires more XP than the previous one
  • There are 16 rank tiers, each with its own name and emoji
  • Higher ranks provide bragging rights and visibility on leaderboards

Animal System

Animals are a core part of PixelMon gameplay:

  • Find animals through hunting and mining
  • Add animals to your farm using /addanimal
  • Each animal has its own stats: health, strength, level, and a trait
  • Animals can be sent on tasks to earn rewards
  • Animals can battle against other users' animals
  • Animals can be bred to create new offspring with inherited traits

Farm System

Your farm is where you keep your animals and build structures:

  • Upgrade your farm to increase animal capacity
  • Build structures to provide benefits to your animals:
    • Animal House: Increases animal capacity
    • Feeding Station: Increases healing effectiveness
    • Training Ground: Increases XP gain for animals
  • Resources from mining are used to build and upgrade structures

Battle System

Battles are a key way to earn coins and XP:

  • User battles: Challenge other users directly
  • Animal battles: Have your selected animal battle another user's animal
  • Battle outcomes are determined by levels, stats, and some randomness
  • Winners earn more coins and XP than losers

Quest and Achievement System

Quests and achievements provide additional goals and rewards:

  • Daily quests reset every 24 hours
  • Weekly quests reset every 7 days
  • Quests involve various activities like battling, collecting items, earning coins, etc.
  • Achievements are one-time accomplishments that reward coins and XP

Clan System

Clans allow you to team up with other players:

  • Create or join a clan
  • Clans have their own level and XP
  • Clan leaders can manage members
  • Clans compete on leaderboards

Tips & Strategies

General Tips

  • Daily Consistency: Make sure to claim your daily reward every day and use the work command whenever it's available.
  • Resource Management: Save your resources for important upgrades rather than spending them on minor improvements.
  • Command Cooldowns: Keep track of command cooldowns and use them as soon as they're available to maximize your earnings.
  • Quest Priorities: Focus on completing quests that align with your natural gameplay to earn extra rewards.

Economy Strategies

  • Balanced Approach: Use a mix of daily, work, battle, and hunting/mining to earn coins consistently.
  • Gambling: Only gamble what you can afford to lose, and consider using it as a last resort when you need coins quickly.
  • Potion Usage: Use coin boost potions during periods of high activity to maximize their effect.

Animal Strategies

  • Trait Selection: Different traits are better for different activities:
    • "Smart" for faster leveling
    • "Brave" or "Aggressive" for battles
    • "Curious" for exploration
  • Breeding: Breed animals with complementary traits and high stats to create stronger offspring.
  • Task Rotation: Send different animals on tasks based on their traits to maximize rewards.

Battle Strategies

  • Level Advantage: Try to battle users who are close to your level for a fair fight and better rewards.
  • Animal Selection: Choose animals with high strength and battle power for animal battles.
  • Healing: Keep your animals healed to ensure they're ready for battles and tasks.

Farm Strategies

  • Upgrade Priority: Upgrade your farm early to increase animal capacity.
  • Structure Focus: Focus on building and upgrading the Animal House first to maximize animal capacity.
  • Resource Gathering: Mine regularly to gather resources for farm upgrades.

Advanced Strategies

  • Clan Collaboration: Join or create a clan to collaborate with other players and share strategies.
  • Potion Timing: Use XP boost potions during periods of high XP gain, such as when claiming quest rewards.
  • Profile Customization: Customize your profile to attract likes and climb the like leaderboard.
